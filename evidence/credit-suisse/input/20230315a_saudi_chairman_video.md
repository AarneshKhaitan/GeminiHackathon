## Transcript (key exchange)

BLOOMBERG INTERVIEWER: Would Saudi National Bank be open to increasing your stake in Credit Suisse?

AMMAR AL KHUDAIRY: The answer is absolutely not, for many reasons outside the simplest reason, which is regulatory and statutory. If we go above 10%, all kinds of new rules kick in, whether be it by our regulator, or the European regulator, or the Swiss regulator. So that is not something that we would consider.

He continued to discuss the bank's investment thesis, expressing confidence in Credit Suisse's restructuring plan and describing the panic as "unwarranted."
