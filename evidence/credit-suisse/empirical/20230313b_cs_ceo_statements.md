# VIDEO: CS CEO Ulrich Koerner Statements

YouTube search: "Ulrich Koerner Credit Suisse March 2023"

Koerner became CS CEO in July 2022, replacing Thomas Gottstein. He had been CEO for approximately 8 months when the acute crisis hit.

## Key appearances

LinkedIn/Internal Video Message (reported March 2023):
Koerner reportedly recorded video messages for CS staff during the crisis week, reassuring employees. Not publicly released but described in media reports.

March 16 Statement (after SNB facility draw):
"This demonstrates decisive action to pre-emptively strengthen Credit Suisse's liquidity. We are taking the necessary steps to protect our clients, our employees, and our stakeholders."

Internal communications (later reported by media) showed Koerner trying to maintain morale while privately acknowledging the severity. Staff were described as demoralized, many updating CVs.

Koerner's public appearances during the crisis week were limited. CS may have calculated that putting Koerner on live TV risked another "absolutely not" moment.
