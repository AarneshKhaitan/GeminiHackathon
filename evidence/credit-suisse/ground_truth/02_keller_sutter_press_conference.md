# VIDEO: Keller-Sutter Press Conference (March 19, 2023)

Swiss Federal Council press conference, evening of March 19, 2023, Bern.

Bloomberg clip: https://www.bloomberg.com/news/videos/2023-03-19/swiss-finance-minister-ubs-buying-credit-suisse-is-not-a-bailout

YouTube search: "Karin Keller-Sutter Credit Suisse UBS press conference March 19 2023"

Conducted partly in German, partly in French, with English translation available in some uploads. Bloomberg, CNBC, BBC carried it live.

Also present: FINMA Director Marlene Amstad, SNB Chairman Thomas Jordan, UBS CEO Ralph Hamers.

## Key Quotes

Keller-Sutter:
"A bankruptcy of Credit Suisse would have had incalculable consequences for the country and the international financial system."

"We had to act fast. We had to act this weekend. There was no other option."

"This is not a bailout. This is a commercial solution."

Thomas Jordan (SNB):
Emphasized the unprecedented liquidity support — up to CHF 200 billion made available.

FINMA Director Amstad:
Explained the AT1 write-down decision and FINMA's legal basis.

Ralph Hamers (UBS CEO):
"This acquisition is attractive for UBS shareholders but, let us be clear, as far as Credit Suisse is concerned, this is an emergency rescue."

## Press conference tone:

Somber and tense. Not a celebration — a crisis management announcement. Officials looked tired, having negotiated since Saturday morning. Journalist questions were sharp, sometimes hostile — particularly about bondholder wipeout, taxpayer exposure, no shareholder vote.
