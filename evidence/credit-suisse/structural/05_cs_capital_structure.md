## Q4 2022 Earnings Release Key Disclosures (February 9, 2023)

From the earnings release:
- **Net loss:** CHF 7.3 billion for full year 2022
- **Q4 net loss:** CHF 1.4 billion
- **Deposit outflows:** CHF 110.5 billion in Q4 2022 (October-December)
- **Revenue decline:** Investment banking revenue down significantly
- **Restructuring costs:** Ongoing charges from the strategic transformation

The CHF 110.5 billion in deposit outflows during Q4 2022 is the critical disclosed figure — representing approximately 20% of the bank's total balance sheet leaving in a single quarter.
