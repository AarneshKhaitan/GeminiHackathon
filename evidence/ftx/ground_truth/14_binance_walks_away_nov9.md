# BREAKING: Binance Walks Away From FTX Acquisition

November 9, 2022. Less than 24 hours after announcing the LOI.

Binance corporate statement:

> "As a result of corporate due diligence, as well as the latest news reports regarding mishandled customer funds and alleged US agency investigations, we have decided that we will not pursue the potential acquisition of FTX.com."

> "In the beginning, our hope was to be able to support FTX's customers to provide liquidity, but the issues are beyond our control or ability to help."

CZ tweeted separately:

> "Sad day. Tried to help, but..."

> "We are shocked by the things that have come to light."

## Market Crash

- BTC crashed from ~$18,500 to below $16,500 (-10%+)
- FTT collapsed from ~$5 to below $3
- SOL dropped another 15-20%
- Total crypto market cap fell by roughly $100 billion in 24 hours
- The relief rally from the previous day was completely reversed and then some
