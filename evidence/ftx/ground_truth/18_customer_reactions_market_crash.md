# Customer Reactions and Crypto Market Crash — Compiled Reports

## Customer Posts (Reddit, Twitter, Discord)

Approximately 1 million+ FTX customers had funds frozen on the platform.

"I had my entire life savings on FTX. $200,000. I can't access any of it."

"I trusted SBF because he testified before Congress and talked about effective altruism. I feel like the biggest idiot in the world."

"Three days ago SBF said assets are fine. I believed him and didn't withdraw. Now I've lost everything."

"I had my business payroll on FTX US. SBF said US entity was solvent. My employees need to get paid next week."

Losses reported ranged from hundreds to millions of dollars. Some users explicitly chose NOT to withdraw because they trusted SBF's "assets are fine" tweet.

## Crypto Market Data

BTC price:
- Nov 6: ~$21,000
- Nov 9: ~$15,700 (two-year low)
- Nov 14: ~$16,300 (partial stabilization)
- Total decline: ~25% in a week

ETH:
- Similar ~25% decline
- Brief dip below $1,100

Total crypto market cap lost approximately $200 billion in the week following CZ's tweet.

## Contagion

- **BlockFi**: $680M exposure to FTX/Alameda. Filed for bankruptcy Nov 28.
- **Genesis Lending**: Halted withdrawals Nov 16 due to FTX exposure. Parent company DCG entered restructuring.
- **Gemini Earn**: Froze withdrawals due to Genesis exposure.
- **Various smaller funds and lenders**: Dozens of crypto firms disclosed FTX exposure.

Total destruction of value (direct + contagion) ran into hundreds of billions.
