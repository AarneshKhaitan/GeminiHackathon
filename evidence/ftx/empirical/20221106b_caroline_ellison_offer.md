# Alameda CEO Offers to Buy Binance's FTT at $22

Caroline Ellison, CEO of Alameda Research, responded to CZ's liquidation announcement on November 6, 2022:

"@caborek if you're looking to minimize the market impact on your FTT sales, Alameda will happily buy it all from you today at $22!"

FTT was trading around $22-23 at the time. Binance held roughly $529 million worth of FTT.

CZ did not take the offer.

Multiple observers noted: if Alameda truly had $529M in cash to buy FTT, why didn't they just buy it on the open market to support the price?

## Crypto Twitter reaction:

Bull camp: "See, Alameda has liquidity. They wouldn't offer to buy if they couldn't afford it."

Bear camp: "If she could afford to buy at $22, she'd be buying on the open market right now. She's not. This is a bluff."

"The fact that she's making this offer publicly instead of just doing it is the tell."

## Outcome

The offer was never executed. FTT dropped below $22 within 24 hours and never recovered. Ellison was later convicted of fraud in connection with the FTX collapse. She cooperated with prosecutors and testified against SBF at trial.
