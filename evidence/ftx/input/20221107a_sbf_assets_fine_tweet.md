# SBF "Assets Are Fine" Tweet — Social Media Archive

## The Tweets

November 7, 2022. Withdrawal requests overwhelming FTX. Sam Bankman-Fried posted on Twitter:

> "FTX is fine. Assets are fine."

Follow-up tweets:

> "FTX has enough to cover all client holdings. We don't invest client assets (even in treasuries). We have been processing all withdrawals, and will continue to do so."

> "A competitor is trying to go after us with false rumors. FTX is fine. Assets are fine."

## What Was Actually Happening

At the time of these tweets:

- FTX had approximately $8 billion shortfall in customer funds
- Customer deposits had been secretly transferred to Alameda Research
- Alameda had lost billions of those funds through trading losses and investments
- FTX was NOT processing all withdrawals — processing had slowed dramatically
- Customer funds had been used to cover Alameda's losses

## Tweet Deletion

The tweet was subsequently deleted.

SBF later acknowledged in a Twitter thread on November 10 that FTX did not have sufficient assets, saying "I f***ed up."
