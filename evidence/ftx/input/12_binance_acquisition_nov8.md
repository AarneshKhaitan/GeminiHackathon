# BREAKING: Binance Signs LOI to Acquire FTX

November 8, 2022.

Changpeng Zhao (CZ) tweeted:

> "This afternoon, FTX asked for our help. There is a significant liquidity crunch. To protect users, we signed a non-binding LOI, intending to fully acquire FTX.com and help cover the liquidity crunch. We will be conducting a full DD in the coming days."

SBF confirmed on Twitter:

> "Things have come full circle, and FTX.com's first, and last, parsec investor is buying us out. We've reached an agreement on a strategic transaction with Binance for FTX.com (pending DD etc.)"

> "Our teams are working on clearing out the withdrawal backlog as is. This will clear out liquidity crunches; all assets will be covered 1:1. This is one of the main reasons we've asked Binance's help. It may take a bit to settle, etc. — we ask for your patience."

## Market Reaction

- BTC recovered about 5% from its intraday low
- Broader crypto market bounced
- FTT stabilized briefly around $5
- Social media shifted to "crisis over, Binance to the rescue"
- Commentators compared it to JPMorgan rescuing Bear Stearns

The LOI was non-binding. Due diligence had not been conducted. Binance had not seen FTX's books.

Less than 24 hours later, Binance walked away.
