# SBF "Assets Are Fine" Tweet

Original URL: https://twitter.com/SBF_FTX/status/1590012124864348160 (deleted)

## The tweet (November 7, 2022):

"1) A competitor is trying to go after us with false rumors. FTX is fine. Assets are fine."

"2) FTX has enough to cover all client holdings. We don't invest client assets (even in treasuries)."

"3) We have been processing all withdrawals, and will continue to do so."

"4) If you'd like, you're welcome to reduce your exposure on our platform. We're fine."

"5) We've been around for years. We process $10-15b/day, and have processed more than that in the past. That takes systems, takes compliance, takes risk management."

## The deletion:

Tweets were deleted during or after November 10-11, when FTX filed for bankruptcy.

## What was true at the time this was posted:

- FTX had approximately $8 billion shortfall in customer funds
- Customer deposits had been secretly transferred to Alameda Research
- Alameda had lost billions through trading losses and investments
- FTX was NOT processing all withdrawals — processing had slowed dramatically
- The claim "we don't invest client assets" was false

SBF later acknowledged on November 10: "I f***ed up."
