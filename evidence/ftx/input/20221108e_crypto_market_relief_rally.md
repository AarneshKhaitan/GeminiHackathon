# Crypto Market Relief Rally — November 8, 2022

CZ announced the Binance-FTX LOI. Market response:

BTC: jumped from ~$17,500 to ~$18,500 (+5-6%)
ETH: up about 7%
FTT: stabilized around $5 (after falling from $22 in two days)
SOL: bounced from lows (Alameda held large SOL positions, SOL had been selling off in sympathy)

Crypto twitter "crisis over" takes:

"Binance acquiring FTX is actually bullish. Consolidation makes the industry stronger."

"CZ is doing what JPMorgan did in 1907 and 2008. This is how capitalism is supposed to work."

"I'm buying the dip. If Binance is willing to put up the money, FTX customers will be made whole."

"SBF made mistakes but the adult in the room (CZ) stepped in. The system works."

Cautious voices:

"It's a non-binding LOI. Wait for the DD. If the hole is $8 billion, CZ isn't going to fill it."

"Two days ago SBF said assets are fine. Now he's selling to his biggest rival. Something doesn't add up."

"JPMorgan buying Bear Stearns didn't prevent Lehman six months later. Don't assume the crisis is over."

The rally lasted about 12 hours. When Binance walked away the next day, crypto crashed hard — BTC eventually fell below $16,000.
